To verify your identity (KYC) on Lipax, please follow these steps:

  • Log into your Lipax account
  • Go to the “Settings” or “Account Verification” section
  • Fill in your personal details (full name, address, etc.)
  • Upload a valid government-issued ID (passport, national ID, etc.)
  • Submit a selfie for identity confirmation if requested
  • Wait for the review – usually completed within 24-48 hours

Make sure all documents are clear and not expired. You will be notified once your KYC is approved.

Lipax is a financial technology company, not a bank. We collaborate with partner banks that hold valid banking licenses to provide essential banking services. The debit cards we offer are issued by these partner banks under licenses from Visa and MasterCard, enabling you to use them at any location that accepts Visa and MasterCard debit cards. Additionally, our platform allows you to easily transfer money, enhancing your financial flexibility.
