Contact Lipax Support

How to contact us?

In your app, from the bottom, select "Account" then "Support" to see all available options. Click on the option you prefer to contact us directly.

We display specific and short guidance for you on topics that are relevant to your experience and your potential issues!

We can only support existing customers when they contact us from the app.

Opening hours

By phone: Most lines are open 24/7, but this may vary by country. Exact opening hours will be displayed in the app.

By email: We answer your request 24/7.

Languages

We currently offer assistance in Arabic, Bengali, Creole, English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Spanish, Tagalog, Twi, Urdu, and Wolof.

Available languages will vary by country, and you will be able to select your preferred language directly when calling us.
